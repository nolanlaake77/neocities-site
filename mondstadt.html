Mondstadt - The City of Wind

From amongst mountains and wide-open plains, carefree breezes carry the scent of dandelions — a gift from the Anemo God, Barbatos — across Cider Lake to Mondstadt, which sits on an island in the middle of the lake.

Mondstadt is the region where the Traveler’s journey in Teyvet with Paimon begins. The starting town City of Mondstadt, also known as City of Wind or Crown of the North, is the heart of the region.

The wind will carry dandelion seeds, songs, and stories far afield, and it also guides gentle Travelers to its realm.

Starfell Valley

Starfell Valley is an area located in Mondstadt. It is considered the most diverse region of Mondstadt, spreading far from the tip of Stormbearer Point to the ends of the Whispering Woods. It is also home to the City of Mondstadt in Cider Lake. The area also consists of abundant lakes and tree variety. The area is where the Knights of Favonius's duties primarily takes place, protecting locals and travelers alike with guards patrolling around the area.

Dragonspine

Dragonspine, historically known as Vindagnyr, is an area located in Mondstadt. It is a vast mountain, engulfed with ancient ruins and snow-capped areas, and also containing the remains of Durin. It was named Vindagnyr prior to Durin's fall. There are some peculiarities with how the snow gathers but never melts on the mountain. Due to its extreme cold, Dragonspine is also one of the most dangerous places in Mondstadt.
